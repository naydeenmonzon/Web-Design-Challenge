WeatherPy

Whether financial, political, or social – data’s true power lies in its ability to answer questions definitively.
“What’s the weather like as we approach the equator?”
Now, we know what you may be thinking: “Duh. It gets hotter…”
But, if pressed, how would you prove it?

In this example, 500+ random cities across the world of varying distance from the equator were pulled to use as a dataset. Once assemebled Matplotlib was used to plot various aspects of the weather vs. latitude based on the following factors:
  • Temperature
  • Cloudlines
  • Wind Speed
  • Humidity
